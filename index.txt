<h2>Tapenade (Olive Spread)</h2>
<p>This is a relly simple dish to prepare and it's always<br>
a big hit at parties.  My father recomends:<br> </p>
<blockquote>

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to<br>
blend.  Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve<br>
it.  In the winter, try seriving it warm."
</blockquote>

<h3>Ingredients</h3>
<ol>
<li>1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes</li>
<li>2 large garlic cloves</li>
<li>2/3 c. kalamata oliver</li>
<li>1 t. capers</li>

<h3>Instructions</h3>
<blockquote>
Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor.  Blend<br>
until as <em>smooth</em> as possible.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
Add capers and olives.   Pulse the motor a few times until they<br>
are incorporated, <em>but still retain some texture</em><br>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
Serve on thin toast rounds with goat chesse and fresh<br>
basil garnish(<em>optional)</em>
